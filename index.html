Preparing young people with skills for life.

At 4th Morpeth, we help over 200 young people develop skills for life.

About 4th Morpeth Scouts

4th Morpeth Scout Group is a large group with over 260 young people and is supported by more than 30 adult volunteers.

Every week, we teach young people skills such as camp craft, first aid, cooking, as well as teamwork. They will work together to earn badges.

It's a great way to have fun, make friends, get outdoors, express their creativity and experience the wider world. There's something for every young person, whatever their ability.

Volunteer

There's lots of ways you can get more involved with Scouts as a parent, carer or family member.

Adult volunteers support the group by participating in a variety of roles including leading our meetings, supporting our camps, maintaining our HQ or being part of our Trustee Board.

If your child wants to join 4th Morpeth, or you are able to support us as a volunteer, please email us at join@4thmorpeth.org.uk. Please note: our group is very popular and you may find there is a waiting list for some of our sections.
